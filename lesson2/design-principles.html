3 Different Design Principles

Simone L. Joseph

PARC: Alignment

League of Legends - Riot Games
https://na.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/

Nothing should be placed on the page arbitrarily. Every element should have some visual connection with another element on the page. This creates a clean and sophisticated look. I love how League of Legends Alignments are set out. Content is either aligned Center or Justified. Content and items are grouped together on the page with the same Alignment. This makes it easy for the user to group information together and see their belonging. This makes reading clean and there is no confusion to users.

PARC: Contrast

Valorant - Riot Games
https://playvalorant.com/en-gb/

Contrast is often the most important visual attraction on a page—it’s what makes a reader look at the page in the first place. It also clarifies the communication. Valorant has great Contrast for me. I feel the background color chosen really makes the font stand out. The color of the font is also in good Contrast with the background and other elements on the page. The font is easy to read and accessible for users.

White Space and Clean Design

Discord
https://discord.com/

The White Space that is used in this design makes it visually appealing and more organized. The white space is not trapped between elements. I feel Discord has created a Clean Design for their users and others that visit their page to learn more about them. Discord has also added enough white space in their design to allow users eye rest and area to breath. The design is neatly created with no elements cramped on the page. Layout is user friendly and easy to navigate around.
