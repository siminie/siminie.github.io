Purpose

The purpose of Simo's Weather Forecast is to provide users with accurate weather forecastings in their hometowns. The website will provide a photo gallery, and links to other webpages which provide weather forecasting information for 3 - 5 other areas. The website will be user friendly to any user that view this site on mobile, tablet or desktop. It will provide an appealing and clean design to navigate through.
