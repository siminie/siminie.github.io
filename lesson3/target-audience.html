Target Audience

This website target audience is for those who are in need of finding out the weather forecast in their area or town. The need for weather forecast could be related to their occupation, hobby, interest etc. This site will provide that need for it's audience and other users who vist it.

Personas

Persona Image Name Job Demographs Technology Motives
Male Persona John Competitive Athlete Young adult, mid-twenties, professionally and casually trains for marathons, Olympians, and other sports events. Uses technology often to track his heartrate, trail run distance and time spent. Wants to check the weather when it is most applicable to go outside on his causal trail runs and exercise.
Female Persona Megan Photographer Married and a new mother to her baby boy, stay at home mother, loves the outdoors, enjoys taking pictures as her job. Feels she has well rounded knowlegde about technology due to her photography job. Would like to check the weather so that she can take her son around when she does does photoshoots.

Scenarios

  1. Can I take my child out with me while I take photos?
  2. Do I need to take a jersey with me on my trail run?
  3. Which town has the best weather for specific photoshoot themes (misty, sunny, overcast etc.)?
  4. Will the weather be better to go running in the evening or in the morning?
  5. What should I wear outside today with the current temperature?
